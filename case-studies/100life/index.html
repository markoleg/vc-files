Development of a corporate website for the "100% Life" clinics network

Challenge: The company aimed to enhance its online presence and increase conversions on its website. Their existing website was outdated, user-unfriendly, and didn't align with modern design standards. Migrating a website from the Russian CMS Tilda to Webflow.

Solution: At Webrarium, we developed a comprehensive plan to improve the online presence and change the business logic of 100%LIFE.

Content Enhancement

We reviewed and updated the website's content, adding detailed information about services, high-quality images, and customer reviews. Additionally, a blog was established to publish articles about the latest company news and expert recommendations from the medical center's doctors.

Strategic Planning:

We conducted an analysis of the target audience and the competitive market. We identified key touchpoints with customers and opportunities to enhance their interaction with the website.

Loading Speed Optimization:

We performed technical optimization on the website to ensure fast page loading. This included image compression, minimizing CSS and JavaScript files.

Interface Redesign

We created a new website design that follows current trends and provides user-friendly navigation. The design was responsive, catering to various devices including mobile and tablets.

SEO Optimization

We conducted comprehensive keyword analysis and optimization to improve the website's search engine rankings. Both on-page and off-page optimization strategies were implemented to increase organic traffic.

After implementing these changes, the 100%LIFE domain fully revived:

Results:

44%
increase in conversions within the first 3 months.
25%
decrease in the bounce rate on the appointment booking page due to the optimized interface.
30%
increase in average time spent on the website, attributed to engaging content

This case study demonstrates how our web studio addressed the client's challenges and achieved excellent results through strategic planning, design, development, and optimization.
