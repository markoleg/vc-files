Increasing the Number of Courses Participants for Ukrainian Catholic University Business School (LvBS) through Contextual Advertising

Situation: The business school has been fostering an open and responsible business community in Ukraine for over 15 years. They aimed to increase brand awareness through targeted contextual advertising and generate more applications and course enrolment's.

Challenges:

  1. Competitive Environment:: The education sector has numerous competitors actively advertising online.
  2. Keyword Selection: Choosing effective keywords to capture the attention of the target audience.
  3. Ad Budget Optimization: Balancing advertising expenditures to achieve maximum ROI.

Strategy:

  1. Audience Research: Studying the interests and behavior of the target audience in the field of professional development.
  2. Keyword Selection: Choosing keywords related to education and professional development.
  3. Ad Creation: Developing engaging ad copy and utilizing visual materials for advertisements.
  4. Audience Segmentation: Segmenting the audience based on interests, age groups, and activity levels.

Execution:

  1. Campaign Launch: Creating ads and configuring targeting parameters for placement on the Google Ads contextual advertising platform.
  2. A/B Testing: Comparing the effectiveness of different ads and keywords to determine the best-performing options.
  3. Optimization: Continuously improving the campaign based on gathered data, including adjusting keywords, modifying ad copy, and refining audience targeting.

Results:

14%
ROI increased by 14% compared to the previous campaign.

This case study example illustrates how the application of contextual advertising along with audience analysis and optimization can lead to positive outcomes for a business.
